ABOUT MARVIN

Marvin is a photographer who was born, grew up and is still based in the beautiful Black Forest in Germany. Already as a young kid he liked to explore and discover the forests around his hometown and started to fall in love with mother nature very immediately. He soon had the wish to capture the remarkable beauty he was surrounded by. And that was the beginning of his photography journey.
He specializes in wild and beautiful landscapes, outdoor shots and travels. In his pictures he captures the unique moments nature has to offer and brings them to an inimitable glow with his own editing style. Besides capturing landscapes, he also has been focused on automotive shots in the past years and practically anything, that fits in nature properly. He has worked with several partners to connect their brand philosophy with nature through pictorial representations.
Along his photography journey he discovered the passion to take people with him on his travels. Therefore he built a huge community on social media following his adventures and taking part in his tours to many different countries, especially through Europe.
